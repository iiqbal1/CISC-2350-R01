The Weeknd

Abel Tesfaye, professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer, songwriter and record producer. The Weeknd has become one of the music industry's leading alternative R&B performers.The Weeknd started recording his efforts while in high school. After dropping out, he continued exploring his interest in music, and released a free download called House of Balloons in 2011. This, along with two more mixtapes, helped push his career forward. He worked with Drake on his 2011 album, Take Care, which featured several tracks from House of Balloons. The Weeknd’s earlier mixtapes were re-released as 2012’s Trilogy, which became a platinum-selling record for the young artist. In 2015, the Weeknd continued his rise to the top with the No. 1 album Beauty Behind the Madness. The Weeknd's 'Starboy' Bows at No. 1 on Billboard 200 With Third-Biggest Debut of 2016
